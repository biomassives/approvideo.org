Approvideo is your one-stop platform for practical, collaborative content on a wide range of sustainable DIY topics. We're building a community where you can learn and share knowledge about:

  • **Shelter:** From temporary shelters to permanent housing, insulation, ventilation, and natural building techniques.
    • Did you know that well-designed natural buildings can significantly reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions?
    • Access to safe and adequate housing is a fundamental human right recognized by the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
  • **Water:** Water filtration, purification, storage, distribution, and urban water solutions.
    • Clean water is essential for life, and access to safe drinking water is a human right.
    • Simple DIY filtration methods can make a huge difference in communities lacking clean water infrastructure.
  • **Waste Management:** Composting, recycling, upcycling, hygiene, zero waste, and advocating for producer responsibility.
    • Effective waste management protects environmental health and conserves valuable resources.
    • By reducing waste and embracing circular economy principles, we can minimize our impact on the planet.
  • **Energy:** Harnessing solar, wind, and hydropower; exploring biogas; and mastering motors, wiring, compressors, HVAC, plumbing, and energy-efficient design.
    • Renewable energy sources like solar and wind power offer clean and sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels.
    • Energy efficiency is key to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting a sustainable future.
  • **Appropriate Technology:** Sustainable solutions for agriculture, construction, healthcare, and community development, including PTSD care and field medicine.
    • Appropriate technology focuses on using simple, accessible solutions to address local needs and challenges.
    • It emphasizes community ownership and empowerment, promoting self-reliance and sustainability.
  • **Agronomy:** Integrated pest management, soil improvement, cover cropping, diversified agriculture, specialty crops, and organic techniques.
    • Sustainable agriculture practices protect biodiversity, enhance soil health, and promote food security.
    • Organic farming methods minimize the use of synthetic chemicals, protecting human and environmental health.
